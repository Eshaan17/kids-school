Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

A kindergarten curriculum focuses on nurturing young minds through play-based learning, foundational skills in literacy and math, and activities that promote creativity, curiosity, and social development.

Elementary School

An elementary school curriculum builds foundational knowledge in core subjects like reading, writing, math, and science while encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and social skills through engaging, hands-on learning.

Middle (10-16) Years

A middle school curriculum focuses on expanding knowledge in core subjects like math, science, language arts, and social studies, while fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and personal growth to prepare students for higher education
